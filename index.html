Making Servers

Sticky
If you want to play with friends over the internet, you will have to port forward your router. The default port is 6969, but you can use any. If you do not know how to do this, a guide can be found here.
*If you are on lan or a school computer, you don't need to port forward, but the other players must connect through your local IP.
